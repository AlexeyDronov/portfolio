# The Philosophy of Subtraction

Minimalism isn't just about white space; it's about removing the unnecessary to allow the essential to speak.

## Focus on Content

When we strip away decorative elements, the content becomes the hero. This requires high-quality typography and well-structured information architecture.

> "Perfection is achieved, not when there is nothing more to add, but when there is nothing left to take away." - Antoine de Saint-Exup√©ry

## Functionality First

Every element on the screen should have a purpose. If it doesn't help the user achieve their goal, it's distraction.
