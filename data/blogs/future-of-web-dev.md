Web development is evolving at a rapid pace. With the introduction of new frameworks and the integration of AI tools, the way we build websites is changing.

## The Rise of AI

AI is becoming an integral part of the developer workflow. From code generation to automated testing, AI tools are helping developers work faster and more efficiently.

## Frameworks to Watch

React continues to dominate, but newer frameworks like Svelte and Solid are gaining traction. It's an exciting time to be a web developer!
